One Step Closer To Safer Internet

With COVID-19 pandemic affecting every aspect of our lives, families across the world have had to adjust to a more online-based lifestyle. This change had the biggest impact on not only adults but children of various ages. Our tool aims to further improve the safety of your children by allowing you to take more control over your browser and putting the power into your hands.

Safety

With our Filters, Group Filters and Use time features you can easily decide on your own.

Accessibility

Childproof is free of charge for everyone available as browser extension.

Anonymity

We collect no data for personal or third-party use about your browsing history.

Safety for your loved ones

Our main goal is to ensure that you are able to control what content is accessible in your browser. For this we've created several ways for you to create and manage filters for websites and time spent using the browser.

With simple user interface you can set block any website in seconds. In addition to filtering, usage limiter and basic overview of past activity is available.

Stay notified

We know that keeping up is hard. That is why we have integrated our extension with PWA technology to give you the option to receive notifications on your phone.

You will be notified when the device attempts to access blocked websites, or when the time limit for the day you’ve set up has ran out.

Childproof was created as a project for Master's thesis for University of Economics in Prague, application has no hidden fees or monetary charges.
